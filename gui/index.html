About USB Speed Utility

What is USB Speed Utility?

USB Speed Utility & Monitor is a user-friendly tool that lets you check how fast your USB flash drives and portable hard disks are. It helps you see if they are performing well, checks how much storage space is left, and keeps an eye on them in the background so you get alerted before they run out of space. It's completely private and runs directly on your computer without sending any data to the cloud.
